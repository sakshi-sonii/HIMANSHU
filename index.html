About Me

I am a design engineer with a passion for 3D printing and a postgraduate student at Conestoga College, where I am studying Applied Manufacturing Management Design Integration 3D Printing. I have a bachelor's degree in Automobile/Automotive Mechanics Technology/Technician from A. D. Patel Institute of Technology, where I learned the fundamentals of manufacturing processes and participated in an electric two-wheeler design competition.

At Ausion Tech, I created innovative and efficient solutions for various industrial projects, using my proficiency in SOLIDWORKS, PTC Creo, and Ultimaker Cura. I designed, modeled, and printed 3D components and assemblies, following the best practices and specifications. I also earned multiple certifications, including the Certified SOLIDWORKS Associate and Professional, and achieved a high score in the International English Language Test. I am eager to explore new possibilities and challenges in the field of 3D printing and enhance my skills and knowledge.
